Here you will find some LaTeX2e packages developed at VTeX. PRESFULL.PDF is paper of the presentation "Inside LaTeX2e kernel" at BachoTeX'99 (GUST Annual Meeting April 30 -- 2 May, 1999, Bachotek). It contains description of some packages based on modified low level LaTeX2e kernel macros.

Different pagestyles for text and float pages.

Columns balancing at last page.

Mixing onecolumn and twocolumn modes at any place of page.

Calculate TeX Bounding Box in points.
marginal.sty
Enlarge free and show lost marginal inserts.
